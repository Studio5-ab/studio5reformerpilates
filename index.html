About Us

Studio 5 is a new boutique Reformer Pilates experience on a term-based schedule, for a handcrafted commitment that works for anyone who is looking to prioritize their health. We’re offering small class sizes for a workout that is sustainable, empowering, and a path for those who want to feel strong. Simply choose the session dates that work for you, and the class times you want to attend, between one and three times a week, for the commitment that works for you. No monthly membership required. Our mission is to create a low pressure wellness experience that respects your time and investment. We believe in creating real community without the crowds, and sustainable wellness that prioritizes balance over burnout.
