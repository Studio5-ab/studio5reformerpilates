About Us

Studio 5 Reformer Pilates offers a new boutique Pilates experience on a term-based schedule, for a handcrafted commitment that works for anyone who is looking to prioritize their health. We’re offering small class sizes for a workout that is sustainable, empowering, and a path for those who want to feel strong. Simply choose the session dates that work for you, and the class times you want to attend, between one and three times a week, for the commitment that works for you.
